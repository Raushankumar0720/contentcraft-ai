# 🚀 ContentCraft AI — AI Powered Viral Content & Digital Marketing Assistant

> Craft smarter. Grow faster. Go viral with AI 🚀

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

In today’s fast-paced digital world, creating **engaging and viral content consistently** is a major challenge.

Content creators, students, and marketers struggle with:

* ❌ Writing attention-grabbing hooks
* ❌ Generating platform-specific content
* ❌ Choosing the right hashtags
* ❌ Understanding what content performs well

As a result, even good ideas fail to reach the right audience.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**ContentCraft AI** is an intelligent AI-powered assistant that helps users create **high-performing, optimized content** for different platforms.

It acts as a **personal digital marketing expert**, generating:

* Viral posts ✍️
* Scroll-stopping hooks 🎣
* Smart hashtags 🔖
* Engagement insights 📊

---

## ✨ What We Built

We developed a **full-stack AI application** that allows users to:

### 🎯 Input:

* Topic (e.g., AI, Fitness, Startups)
* Platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube)
* Target Audience (Developers, Students, Founders)

### ⚡ Output:

* Viral-ready content
* Hook lines
* Optimized hashtags
* Engagement-based **Viral Score**

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User enters topic, platform, and audience
2. Backend sends request to AI model
3. AI generates:

   * Hook
   * Content
   * Hashtags
4. System evaluates content and assigns a **Viral Score**
5. User gets ready-to-post optimized content 🚀
